Welcome to HiTNEX-X

Kobaryo started activities in 2010. In addition to his music activities, he also works on illustrations and designs under the name of "窓壁九真好".

While operating the non-genre music label [HiTNEX TRAX], Japan's largest hardcore techno label [HARDCORE TANO*C], He is also a member of the speedcore specialty label [Psycho Filth Records].

He has a wide range of styles and is good at putting together various elements into one song. While mainly producing HARDCORE, he uses various names such as EDM, TRANCE, HARDSTYLE, UK HARDCORE, NOISE, etc. Taking advantage of his natural dexterity, he is working on songs of various genres.

In SPEEDCORE, he pulled out a large amount of sampling and synths in various directions. He specializes in music that develops rapidly and chaotically, with a phantasmagoric sound.

In recent years, in addition to providing music for rhythm games and vocalists, BGM production for games and videos, etc. We are expanding the range of activities regardless of the scene.
